Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about contacting us

How quickly do you respond to emails?

We aim to respond to all emails within 2 hours during working hours. For emergencies, please use our 24/7 helpline for immediate assistance.

What should I do in a blood emergency?

Call our emergency helpline immediately at 1800-999-9999. Our team will coordinate with the nearest blood bank and activate our emergency donor network.

Can I visit your office without appointment?

Yes, you can visit during working hours. However, for specific queries or meetings, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure the right person is available.

Is live chat available 24/7?

Yes, our live chat support is available 24/7. In case of high volume, you might experience a short wait time. For emergencies, please use phone support.

How do I report a issue with donation?

Please email our donor support team at donors@life4u.in or call 1800-DONOR-99. We take all concerns seriously and will respond within 1 hour.
